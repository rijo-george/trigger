How it works

1

Hover the notch

The panel drops down from your MacBook notch. Type what you need to do.

2

Pick a trigger

Choose when: a time, an app, a location, when you're back, before you leave. One tap.

3

Forget about it

The notch fires your intention at the right moment. Done or snooze. That's it.
